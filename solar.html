Solar Power

What Do Solar Panels Look Like?

Information

A solar panel is a technological device that converts sunlight into electricity, forming energy. This process is possible through the use of photovoltaic cells. Sunlight activates solar panel, energy from the sunlight is absorbed by the PV cells in the panel. This energy creates electrical charges that move in response to an internal electrical field in the cell, causing electricity to flow.
Solar panels are more beneficial to the environment rather than fossil fuels, such as coals, oils, and natural gases - as they do not release harmful pollutants or gas emissions, like carbon dioxide into the air and water.

Cost

Most recent information put out by MarketWatch show that a 2,000-square-foot home will likely cost around $20,000 to install solar panels. On the same hand, a typical solar PV panel in 2022 is rated between 380 to 420 watts and will cost between $200 to $315. With addition of other components such as, labor, design, permits, roof mounted system, ground mounted system, etc., will bump the price to run about $1000-$1200 per panel. However, bearing in mind your location and eligibility, certain incentives and solar rebates can be granted to reduce or even eliminate such costs.

Tax rebate information

Fortunately, Delmarva Power, DEMEC and Delaware Electric Cooperative all offer solar rebate programs! Any Delmarva Power customer can qualify for a Green Energy Grant to go solar. The grant pays out $700 per kilowatt (kW), up to a maximum amount of $6,000 for your home solar installation! For example, on an average sized 6kW system, you can expect a rebate of $3,600. The DEMEC green energy program rebate pays out $1,000 per kW, up to a maximum of $3,500. You’d max out that incentive with a small-ish 3.5kW system. The Delaware Electric Cooperative’s green energy rebate pays out $500 per kW, up to a maximum of $2,500. So, to get maximum credit, you’d need to install a 5kW system.

Clean/green energy practices

Everyday, solar panels exercise clean and green energy practices through the creation of renewable and infinite energy sources and electricity. Due to the fact that sunlight is a renewable resources, it does not release airborne pollutants; avoiding any harmful greenhouse gas emissions to be emitted into the atmosphere. Therefore, is eco-friendly.
